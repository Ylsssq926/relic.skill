# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

本公约参考 Contributor Covenant 2.1，并结合 relic.skill 的使用场景补充了专属红线。  
This code of conduct is adapted from Contributor Covenant 2.1, with additional rules for relic.skill.

## 我们的承诺 / Our Pledge

我们承诺为每一位参与者提供开放、友善、尊重的社区环境，不因年龄、体型、可见或不可见的残障、族群、性别特征、性别认同与表达、经验水平、教育程度、社会经济地位、国籍、外貌、种族、宗教或性取向而区别对待。  
We pledge to make participation in our community a respectful and harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

我们也承诺：在 relic.skill 社区内，讨论和创作与 Relic 相关的内容时，始终尊重被蒸馏对象的隐私、尊严与边界。  
We also pledge to respect the privacy, dignity, and boundaries of the people or subjects being distilled into Relics.

## 鼓励的行为 / Positive Behavior

以下行为有助于建立健康社区：

- 用善意和耐心交流，尤其是在 review、讨论和纠错时。
- 接受建设性反馈，并愿意根据证据修正观点。
- 在提交样例、日志或数据片段前先做脱敏处理。
- 尊重被蒸馏对象的隐私和尊严，不把真实痛苦当作展示素材。
- 把讨论聚焦在问题、设计和证据，而不是人身判断。
- 在模板设计中明确授权条件、安全边界和不适用场景。

The following behaviors help build a healthy community:

- Showing empathy and patience during review, discussion, and correction.
- Accepting constructive feedback and revising ideas when evidence changes.
- Redacting examples, logs, and data snippets before sharing them.
- Respecting the privacy and dignity of distilled subjects.
- Focusing discussion on the problem, design, and evidence rather than personal attacks.
- Making consent requirements, safety boundaries, and out-of-scope cases explicit in templates.

## 不可接受的行为 / Unacceptable Behavior

以下行为在 relic.skill 社区中不可接受：

- 骚扰、羞辱、侮辱、威胁、恶意挑衅或持续性的人身攻击。
- 发布、索要或传播他人的私密信息，包括聊天记录、照片、录音、地址、联系方式等可识别内容。
- 未经授权蒸馏他人，或鼓励他人绕过授权协议去蒸馏现实个体。
- 使用 Relic 进行骚扰、跟踪、冒充、操控、诈欺或其他伤害行为。
- 提交、请求、传播或协助任何针对政治人物的蒸馏内容、模板、数据或讨论。
- 把社区空间当作发布仇恨内容、色情骚扰、歧视性言论或暴力威胁的场所。
- 明知样例包含真实私密数据仍继续公开分享。

The following behaviors are unacceptable in the relic.skill community:

- Harassment, humiliation, insults, threats, sustained disruption, or personal attacks.
- Publishing, requesting, or spreading private identifiable information such as chats, photos, recordings, addresses, or contact details.
- Distilling someone without authorization, or encouraging others to bypass the consent protocol.
- Using a Relic for harassment, stalking, impersonation, manipulation, fraud, or other harmful acts.
- Submitting, requesting, sharing, or assisting any distillation content, template, data, or discussion targeting political figures.
- Using community spaces to post hateful, sexually harassing, discriminatory, or violent content.
- Publicly sharing real private data after being told it is sensitive.

## 执行责任 / Enforcement Responsibilities

项目维护者有责任解释并执行本行为准则。对于违反规则的 issue、PR、评论、示例、数据片段或其他贡献，维护者可以删除、编辑、拒绝或要求修改。  
Community maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this code of conduct. They may remove, edit, reject, or request changes to issues, pull requests, comments, examples, data snippets, or other contributions that violate these rules.

## 适用范围 / Scope

本准则适用于所有项目空间，包括但不限于：GitHub issue、pull request、discussion、commit message、文档、示例数据、QQ群以及代表 relic.skill 社区发言的公开场合。  
This code of conduct applies within all project spaces, including GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, commit messages, documentation, example data, the QQ group, and public spaces when someone is representing the relic.skill community.

## 处理方式 / Reporting and Enforcement

如果你遇到骚扰、隐私侵犯、未经授权蒸馏、冒充使用或其他违规行为，请私下联系项目维护者。你可以通过 GitHub 私信仓库维护者，或在 QQ 群 **1098169092** 联系管理员。请不要在公开场合继续扩散受害者的私密数据。  
If you experience harassment, privacy violations, unauthorized distillation, impersonation, or other misconduct, contact the maintainers privately. You may reach the repository maintainers through GitHub private contact or by messaging the admins in QQ group **1098169092**. Please do not amplify the victim's private data in public while reporting the issue.

维护者会尽快审查报告，并根据情节采取措施，包括但不限于：

1. 口头或书面提醒
2. 要求删除、修改或脱敏相关内容
3. 暂时限制参与项目空间
4. 永久移除社区参与资格

Maintainers may respond with one or more of the following actions:

1. A warning or clarification
2. A request to delete, revise, or redact content
3. A temporary restriction from project spaces
4. A permanent ban from community participation

## relic.skill 专属补充条款 / relic.skill Specific Rules

为避免项目偏离初衷，以下条款优先适用：

1. **尊重隐私与尊严**：任何与 Relic 相关的样例、模板、讨论，都必须把被蒸馏对象当作真实存在的人或生命来对待。
2. **授权先于蒸馏**：未获得明确授权时，不得蒸馏可识别的现实个体。
3. **禁止骚扰与冒充**：不得把 Relic 用作骚扰工具、冒充工具或情感操控工具。
4. **不蒸馏政治人物**：项目不接收政治人物相关的蒸馏内容、模板、示例或 PR。

To keep the project aligned with its purpose, the following rules take priority:

1. **Respect privacy and dignity**: treat every distilled subject as a real person or living being.
2. **Consent before distillation**: do not distill identifiable real individuals without explicit authorization.
3. **No harassment or impersonation**: do not use Relics as tools for abuse, impersonation, or manipulation.
4. **No political figures**: the project does not accept distillation content, templates, examples, or PRs involving political figures.
